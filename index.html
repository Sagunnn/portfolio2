About

I am currently a 4th-year Ph.D. student at Kwangwoon University in the Advanced Sensor and Energy Research (ASER) Lab. My research focuses on developing innovative wearable sensors and energy harvesting technologies, leveraging cutting-edge machine learning techniques, including pattern recognition, to enhance sensor capabilities. Over the past 3 years, I have co-authored 15+ peer-reviewed papers, contributing significantly to the fields of wearable sensors, triboelectric nanogenerators, energy harvesting, and self-powered systems. Additionally, I am involved in developing applications and interfaces for newly created sensor technologies, aiming to integrate them seamlessly into real-world IoT and Industry 4.0 applications.
